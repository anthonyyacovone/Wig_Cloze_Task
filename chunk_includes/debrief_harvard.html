DEBRIEFING:

What is this study about?

  • This study examines how easily people predict different nouns given previous sentence context.

  • People often have a difficult time predicting nouns at the beginning of a sentence.

Imagine predicting the noun 'movies' given the sentences below:

1a. John has strong opinions about television shows but even more so about ___movies___
1b. John likes ___movies___

  • It is more difficult to accurately predict nouns with less context (1b).

  • With more context, people can better anticipate upcoming nouns (1a).

How is the study conducted?

In this task, you saw a story from beginning to end, sentence by sentence. We are looking to see how predictions differ across nouns in these sentences.

A few more questions:

Did you think the story was interesting and engaging?


Overall, how predictable do you think the words were?
How well do you think you performed on this task?
