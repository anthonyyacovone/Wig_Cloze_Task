STUDY INFORMATION


Please read this consent agreement carefully before deciding whether to participate in this study.

Purpose of the research:

To complete various English sentences from a story by filling in the next word in the blank.

What you will do in this research:

In this experiment, you will be shown a Moth story, which is a story that was originally told out loud by a person. The story is about cancer, but it's a light and humurous story. You are to complete the blank(s) in each sentence to the best of your ability. This task is estimated to take about 30 minutes.

Risks:

There are no anticipated risks associated with participating in this study.

Benefits:

There are no anticipated benefits to the participant beyond whatever amusement you happen to derive from the task.

Selection of participants:

To participate in this study, you must be 18 years of age and a native speaker of American English.

Compensation:

Your compensation for completing this task will be $3.00. This is what you agreed to when selecting this Amazon Turk HIT.

Confidentiality:

Your participation in this study will remain confidential. We do not have access to your identity.

Participation and withdrawal:

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary, and you may quit at any time without penalty or loss of benefit to which you may otherwise be entitled.
